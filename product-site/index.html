The problem

Schools run on paper, Excel, and WhatsApp

No single source of truth. Slow report cards. No real analytics. Parents kept in the dark.

📋

Paper registers

Daily attendance recorded by hand, re-entered later, easy to lose and impossible to analyze.

📊

Scattered spreadsheets

Marks and fee records living in disconnected Excel files with no shared view across staff.

💬

WhatsApp as infrastructure

Parent updates, notices, and teacher notes buried in chat threads instead of a real record.

The solution

One login. Everyone sees exactly what they need.

Role-based access built in from day one — enforced at the API level, not just the UI.

Student Their own view, always up to date
  • Calendar-style attendance history — present, absent, at a glance
  • Exam results by term (Mid Term 1, Quarterly, and more)
  • Timetable, notices, and teacher notes in one place
  • Fee status — paid, pending, and details
  • Direct messaging to teachers and the principal
Teacher Less admin, more teaching
  • Mark attendance for your class in under a minute
  • Enter marks for your subject — even across other classes when granted access
  • Manage your student roster, add and edit records directly
  • Create exam names and assign subject-wise entry access
  • Send notes to parents and students
Principal / Admin A school-wide view, in real time
  • Attendance and exam performance across every class
  • Student lookup by class — helpful the moment a parent walks in
  • Fee collection status across the school
  • Broadcast messages to students or teachers
  • Targeted messages to a specific class teacher, with replies
